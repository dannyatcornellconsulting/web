Danny Cornell

Hi. Im Danny. I do 'IT'. I've been doing IT for nearly 25 years now and have watched a vast landscape of technolgies and services come, go and evolve. I began this love affair back in the traditional on-premises datacentre of a company that offered hosting, DR/BR facilities, End of Day processing for international and offshore banks, IT training facilities and secure offsite storage for tape backups, yes "TAPES"!!

The past 6 years have seen seen my skillsets shift into the cloud space with more and more consumers taking advantage of the landscape and convenience that cloud providers have to offer. These skills range from offering fully native cloud and hybrid on-premises solutions along with the traditional requirements of managing physical network devices and Windows operating systems.

Multi Cloud Connectivity

Why only have one? Connecting Azure, AWS and GCP together to form a single cloud network. This is achieved by using the native cloud providers services such as Azure Virtual WAN, AWS Transit Gateway and GCP Cloud Interconnect, all with support for either your existing routing protocols (BGP) or staic routes to expand your WAN.

Migration to the Cloud

Moving or even just expanding into the cloud can give your restricted and sometimes constrained on-premises network a new lease of life. Full migration isnt always the answer or even required but you open up so many more services and solutions.
